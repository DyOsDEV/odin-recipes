Cookies

Description

Delight in the comforting aroma and perfect balance of sweetness in these fresly baked homedade cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of butter.
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar.
  • 1 cup of brown sugar.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 teaspoon of vainilla extract.
  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda.
  • 2 cups of chocolate chips.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350ºF
  2. Cream together butter, sugars, eggs, and vainilla.
  3. Sift together flour, salt, and baking soda.
  4. Mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  5. Add chocolate chips if desired.
  6. Form dough balls on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
  8. Cool on a wire rack.
