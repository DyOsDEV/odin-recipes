Description

A refreshing blend of ripe banana, frozen strawberries, and creamy yogurt, kissed with a hint of honey for a delightful sip.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana.
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries.
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt.
  • 1/2 cup milk.
  • 1 tbsp honey.
  • Ice cubes.

Steps

  1. Blend banana, frozen strawberries, yogurt, and milk.
  2. Add honey for sweetness if desired.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Add ice cubes if a colder consistency is preferred.
  5. Pour and enjoy your refreshing fruit smoothie!
